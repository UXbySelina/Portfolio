BluePigg

Blue Pigg is a prototype of a financing app. I developed it from concept to prototype during my UX course at CareerFoundry. In the process, I could train my skills at all the different stages, from hand-drawn sketches to high fidelity wireframes.

BluePigg Design and Prototyping

The project started with defining the problem statement and the 5 W’s. Then, I followed the design thinking process to conceptualise the prototype. This included creating personas, user journeys, task flows, the information architecture, as well as conducting interviews. Going from low to high fidelity wireframes, I added a design language document and refined the prototype further with usability testing.
