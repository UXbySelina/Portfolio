Behind the Screens: Selina's UX Odyssey

My Story

My name is Selina Weißkircher, UX researcher and designer. I became interested in the field while studying Theater- Film- and Mediascience at the University of Vienna. I then received my training in UX design from Career Foundry. In 2023 I started working as a UX designer at the “Fonds Soziales Wien”.

Besides people I am also interested in man’s best friend - cats. Spending time with my two cuddly tigers and dancing are my favourite activities to relax. Even though both are just for fun, I can translate much of my experience as a dancer to my work as both require sound understanding of space and movement.

My interests are AI and virtual reality. I would be happy to explore both areas in future work!
